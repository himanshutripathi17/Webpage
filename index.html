Materials for House Construction

  • Wood
  • Brick
  • Concrete
  • Steel
  • Glass

Wood

Wood is a versatile and sustainable material used in many types of construction. It is used for framing, flooring, and finishing.

Brick

Brick is a durable and fire-resistant material often used in walls and facades.

Concrete

Concrete is a strong and durable material used for foundations, floors, and walls. It can be molded into various shapes.

Steel

Steel is a strong and flexible material used for structural support in buildings.

Glass

Glass is used for windows, doors, and facades. It allows natural light to enter the building.
